About Us

AS-AVTO is an online platform for car spare parts and accessories. Our main goal is to provide customers with quality products, a wide range, and affordable prices. Our platform offers a convenient and transparent shopping experience for both individual users and sellers.

  • Thousands of products and various categories
  • Spare parts from local and international brands
  • Product search and filtering by car compatibility
  • Ability for sellers to add and manage their own products
  • Online ordering, cart, and payment functions
  • Professional support and fast delivery
  • User and seller profiles, balance and debt tracking
  • Product liking, rating, and review system
  • Special sections for new arrivals, best sellers, most liked, and highest valued products
  • Admin and seller panel, product and order management
  • Modern design fully compatible for mobile and desktop

The AS-AVTO team is constantly working to improve service quality, expand product variety, and ensure customer satisfaction. You can contact us to share your suggestions and feedback.
