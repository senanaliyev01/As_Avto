Privacy Policy

Protecting the personal data and privacy of users on the AS-AVTO platform is important to us. Below you will find information about how your data is collected, stored, and used.

Collected Information

  • During registration: username, email, phone number, address
  • During orders and purchases: product and order information, payment and delivery details
  • Profile and user activity: liked products, ratings, products you have added
  • User and seller profile, balance and debt information
  • Site activity and user statistics

Use of Information

  • Account creation and user identification
  • Order processing and delivery
  • Customer support and service improvement
  • Statistical analysis and platform development
  • Providing information as required by law

Data Protection

  • Data is stored on secure servers and not shared with third parties
  • Users can only access their own account and personal information
  • Users have the right to delete or update their information at any time
  • Passwords and sensitive data are stored in encrypted form

For additional questions or to request data deletion, you can contact us.
